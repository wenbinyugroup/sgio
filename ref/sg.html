class sgio.core.sg.StructureGene(name='', sgdim=None, smdim=None, spdim=None)#

Bases: object

A finite element level structure gene model in the theory of MSG.

Parameters:

  • name (str, optional) – Name of the SG.

  • sgdim (int, optional) – Dimension of the SG.

  • smdim (int, optional) – Dimension of the material/structural model. Beam (1), plate/shell (2), 3D continuum (3).

  • spdim (int, optional) – Dimension of the space.
